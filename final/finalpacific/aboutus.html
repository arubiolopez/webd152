About Us

Pacific Trails Resort was founded by a group of nature enthusiasts, who wanted others to experience and view nature in the same lens as them. Their love for nature led them to create this immersive resort where people not only get the chance to be in nature and relax but through immersion are also able to value it.

Meet the Team

Aurora:Founder

Aurora is the founder of the resort. Prior to founding the Pacific Trails Resort she was a marine biologist who conducted research in the Monterey Bay. Through her experience there she gained great leadership skills, but also the drive to wanting to share her knowledge and passion for nature with others. She now enjoys managing this resort where everyone gets to enjoy it in their own way (hiking, kayaking, etc.).

Ryle:Co-founder

Ryle is the co-founder of the resort. Prior to founding the Pacific Trails Resort he was a environmental science teacher who focused on bringing awareness to issues like global warming. This gave him the drive to want to bring more awareness to the issue on a larger scale. With the resort not only do people enjoy a comfortable stay but through activities they learn to value the planet, creating awareness in a fun, relaxing, and unique way.
